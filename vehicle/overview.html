Overview of hardware and software systems

  • A compass, a GPS receiver, a stereo camera and ultrasonic sensors were connected to the PC-based vehicle.
  • A GPS receiver was connected to a laptop.
  • The laptop executed two programs: main station and reference station.
  • The purposes of main station program were path planning for the vehicle and monitoring the vehicle.
  • The purpose of reference station program was sending its GPS raw data to the vehicle, and the vehicle used these data and vehicle's own GPS raw data to calculate the position.
